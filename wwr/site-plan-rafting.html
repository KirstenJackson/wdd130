Overview

Purpose

This website exists to bring more customers to the Rapids company. Currently awareness is only spread through word of mouth, and appointments have to be booked over the phone. Having a website will allow customers to learn, explore, and make reservations with ease. The decrease of resistance will increase the number of people that come and enjoy the experience that Rapids has to offer. The increase in customers will naturally increase our revenue.

Audience

Most current customers are avid white water rafters. Their enthusiasm is definitely appreciated, but we would like to increase our audience. Given the location of Rapids is relatively close to a college campus and a residential area, our focus will be on college students and young families. None of these groups are particularly wealthy, and are usually busy during the week. The financial situations of the memebers of these groups have a great range. However, all need fun and accessible things to do. They currently don't see white water rafting as an accesible activity. They need a place to get away and enjoy life before getting back to their weekly routines. Devices used to access the site will usually be a mobile device, as most college students and parents are very busy.
